Hi! My name is Qianwen Shen, you can call me Sylvia. I'm from China, and I'm currently studying for a master's degree in computing at Anu. Regarding the web topic requested by this assignment, I would like to introduce myself and some of my hobbies. The first thing I want to introduce is my photography hobby. I love photography. When I have free time, I will pick up my camera and go shooting on the streets to record some interesting moments. Every time I see these photos of scenery and portraits, it reminds me of some unique memories. When I travel to other places, I will also use my camera to record some of the customs of other places. So I will put some photos I took on this web page and attach some stories related to these photos. In addition, I will also post some videos I edited on this website. I'm also a big movie buff and I'd love to share a 'My Top Movie Picks' section. I'll list some of my favourites and pick two or three standout movies from different categories, like science fiction, thrillers, historical dramas, romances, etc. For each movie, I'll include a few film screenshots and some of the most memorable lines. In addition, I prefer crystal bracelets, so I can also share some about these different crystals and the energy of these crystals on this website. And I also made a lot of crystal bracelets for my good friends, so I can also share some related stories and photos.
